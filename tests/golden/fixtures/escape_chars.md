# Escape Characters

Asterisks used as \*markers\* in text.

Underscores in variable\_names and file\_paths.

Square brackets like \[option\] and \[flag\] in docs.

Parentheses for grouping \(a + b\) in expressions.

Backslash \\ is the escape character itself.

Mixed: this has \*asterisks\*, \_underscores\_, and \[brackets\].
