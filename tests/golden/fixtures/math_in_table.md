# Math in Tables

Inline math expressions inside table cells.

## Physics Formulas

| Symbol | Formula | Meaning |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Energy | $E = mc^2$ | Mass-energy equivalence |
| Force | $F = ma$ | Newton's second law |
| Area | $A = \pi r^2$ | Area of a circle |

## Mixed Math and Annotations

| Function | Expression | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Quadratic | $f(x) = x^2$ | **Parabola shape** |
| Square root | $g(x) = \sqrt{x}$ | *Positive domain only* |
| Logarithm | $h(x) = \ln(x)$ | `domain: x > 0` |
