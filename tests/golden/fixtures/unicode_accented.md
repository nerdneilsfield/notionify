# Café and Résumé

## European Languages

The French word for "hello" is **bonjour**.

In Spanish, *hola* is common and the currency was **peseta** (~~now replaced by euro~~).

German has umlauts: ü, ö, ä, and the sharp s: ß.

Norwegian uses æ, ø, å — as in **Hei, verden!**
