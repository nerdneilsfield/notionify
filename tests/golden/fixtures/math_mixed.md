# Mixed Math Document

A document combining inline math, block math, and regular text to test
that all math strategies work together without interfering.

## Inline Math in Text

The Pythagorean theorem states that $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$ for right triangles.

Euler's identity $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$ is considered beautiful.

## Block Math

$$
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}
$$

## Mixed Paragraph

When computing $f(x) = x^2 + 2x + 1$, note that it factors as $(x+1)^2$.

The following formula for the normal distribution is important:

$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
$$

The mean is $\mu$ and the standard deviation is $\sigma$.
